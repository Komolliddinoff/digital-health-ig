### Note

Standardized pre-test instructions (e.g., fasting, dietary restrictions, medication adjustments, and preparation requirements) are essential for:

- ensuring proper patient preparation  
- improving consistency of clinical workflows  
- enabling accurate interpretation of laboratory results  
- supporting interoperability across healthcare systems
